Schnazzy Schnauzers: Humble Beginnings



Welcome to Schnazzy Schnauzers -- A somewhat-biased canine store!

Schnazzy Schnauzers began whenever a fluffy little brat named Viktor found me. I had a schnauzer growing up, but she wasn't very... fancy, so to speak. Viktor, however, loves to dress up.
That said, all of these outfits were designed with him in mind! However, we both think that they would be cute on any pooch!


Since schnauzers come in mini, standard, and giant, so do their outfits! Three sizes fit most. :)
